
## Question 1: Paraphrase (summarize) the video in a few (1 or 2) lines.(Grit Video)

Grit means having strong passion and continuing to work towards your goals for a long time without giving up. Success is not only about talent, but about consistent effort and determination even when things are difficult.

## Question 2: Paraphrase (summarize) the video in a few (1 or 2) lines.(Introduction to Growth Mindset)

A growth mindset means believing that intelligence and skills can be improved through practice and learning. Challenges, mistakes, and feedback help us grow and become better over time.

---

## Question 3: What is Internal Locus of Control? What is the key point in the video?

Internal Locus of Control means believing that your success and failure depend mainly on your own actions and decisions. The key point of the video is that when you take responsibility for your results, you stay more motivated and focused.

---

## Question 4: Key Points to Build a Growth Mindset

- Believe that abilities can improve with effort and discipline 
- Accept and face challenges  
- Learn from mistakes  
- Take feedback positively  
- Always keep trying after failure  
- Focus on effort and improvement  
- Don't think negatively
